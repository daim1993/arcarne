# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creative directors, brand and campaign leads, studios, recruiters, and potential clients evaluating Diaa Eddin Ahmed Ibrahim for motion design, 3D art, storyboard, visual identity, and immersive digital work. They may arrive with only a few minutes to assess originality and craft, so the experience must make the strongest work discoverable immediately while rewarding deeper exploration.

## Product Purpose

Arcarne is a cinematic, interactive portfolio universe for DAIM. It turns Diaa's body of work, experience, capabilities, analytical foundation, and contact paths into a spatial narrative rather than a conventional resume. Success means a visitor quickly understands the combination of logic, motion, 3D, and story, remembers the experience, discovers relevant projects without friction, and can begin a conversation with confidence.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, precise, immersive. The voice is confident, curious, technically sharp, premium, experimental, and story-led. The emotional arc should move from wonder, to orientation, to discovery, to trust, and finally to action.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a PDF resume, a generic portfolio grid, a paragraph-heavy biography, a standard About / Work / Contact template, or an interchangeable neon sci-fi demo. Avoid flat repeated cards, gratuitous glass panels, decorative gradients without structure, unverified vanity metrics, unreadable experimental typography, excessive scroll hijacking, and effects that obscure the work.

## Design Principles

1. Make the work the brightest object in the universe.
2. Turn navigation into discovery without sacrificing orientation or speed.
3. Use motion to reveal, guide, reward, explain, or transition, never as ambient noise alone.
4. Express the differentiator: analytical precision engineered into cinematic imagination.
5. End wonder with clarity by making project details and contact actions unmistakable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for readable content and controls. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, touch input, strong contrast, screen-reader labels, reduced-motion preferences, and a usable non-WebGL or low-power fallback. Do not rely on hover, color, sound, or motion alone to communicate meaning. Keep audio opt-in and preserve user control.
